## ðŸ”¬ **The Science of "Getting Stuck"**

### **Why Getting Stuck is GOOD:**

**Research shows:**
- Struggling to solve a problem strengthens neural pathways
- "Desirable difficulty" creates deeper learning
- Easy learning = shallow retention
- Hard learning = deep retention

### **The Optimal Struggle:**

**Good stuck (Keep going):**
- "I know what I want to do, not sure how"
- "I've tried 3 things, time to try a 4th"
- "Let me Google this specific error"

**Bad stuck (Time to break/ask for help):**
- "I have no idea what's even happening"
- "I've been staring at this for 2 hours"
- "I want to quit programming"

### **The 15-Minute Rule:**

1. Get stuck
2. Try solving for 15 minutes
3. Still stuck? Take 5-min break
4. Come back, try 15 more minutes
5. Still stuck? Look up answer or ask for help
6. Learn from solution
7. Rebuild it yourself

**The key:** Struggle first, then look up. Never look up first.
